The Architecture of the Evening

A Singular
Destination.

We built The Orange Pair to solve a persistent dilemma: the disruption of moving between locations as an evening unfolds. Here, your entire night transitions smoothly under one roof.

Positioned elegantly on the water's edge, the venue pairs unobstructed harbor access with absolute interior discretion, ensuring privacy remains completely uncompromised.

Guest Policy

  • • Members are welcome to sponsor guests subject to capacity.
  • • Sponsoring guests pay the applicable club admission fee.
  • • Guests enter uniformly through the main entrance architecture.
  • • Members arriving with guests must accompany them through arrival.
  • • Members arriving alone retain exclusive use of the Members Entrance.

Locker Program

Multiple locker dimensions are built across the space to accommodate custom collections. Allocations clear sequentially based on:

  • 1. Founder's Circle Members
  • 2. Cigar Members
  • 3. Remaining structural availability

Lockers are handled as an optional privilege and are not guaranteed with standard membership.
