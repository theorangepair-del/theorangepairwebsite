House Protocols

Operational Protocols

Is membership mandatory to access the club?
We operate primarily as a private member ecosystem. Gated table reservations and specific lounge access are prioritized exclusively for active membership holders and their sponsored guests.
Are cigars required to be smoked by guests?
No. While our custom cigar program and specialized ventilation lounge spaces serve as core components of our identity, they remain entirely optional for guests prioritizing our dining, mixology, or entertainment programs.
What is the explicit camera and privacy policy?
To protect high-profile members, yacht owners, and executives, recording or photographing other members within our club walls without express permission is strictly prohibited. Discretion is absolute.
Can guests arrive by maritime transit or yacht?
Yes. Our waterfront footprint provides convenient maritime access points, details of which are coordinated directly through the concierge team during reservation clearing.
